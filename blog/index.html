课题组承担建设的IEEE华科学生分会与武汉联合分会荣获2025年IEEE集成电路芯片方向学会重要奖项

Posted on July 15, 2025

Our lab is proud to announce that the IEEE Student Branch at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) and the Wuhan Joint Branch, which our group helped establish, have been awarded significant recognition at the 2025 IEEE Integrated Circuit (IC) Design Conference. This prestigious award recognizes our outstanding contributions to fostering the next generation of IC design professionals and our commitment to building strong academic communities.

The achievement would not have been possible without the dedicated effort of our students and collaborators. We look forward to continuing our efforts in promoting IC education and research in the region.

Posted on June 28, 2025

We are delighted to share that Mr. Bingqiang Liu, the first PhD student mentored by our group, has been recognized as an Outstanding Graduate of Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Additionally, he has received an offer from HiSilicon (Huawei's semiconductor subsidiary) for a challenging position in chip design.

Bingqiang joined our group as an undergraduate and demonstrated exceptional aptitude and work ethic throughout his academic journey. His achievement reflects not only his personal excellence but also the effectiveness of our research training program. We wish him continued success in his career at HiSilicon.
